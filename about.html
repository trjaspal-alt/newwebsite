Who We Are

Building Skills, Confidence, and Livelihood Opportunities

CSC E-SEWA SOCIETY, founded in 2015 and headquartered in Punjab, is a mission-driven organization dedicated to skill development, education, employment support, rural empowerment, consultancy, and capacity building.

The organization works to empower individuals with job-ready skills, industry-relevant training, and sustainable livelihood opportunities. Through its regional presence in Gurdaspur, Punjab, CSC E-SEWA SOCIETY collaborates with government bodies, partner agencies, training institutions, and communities to create measurable social and economic impact.
