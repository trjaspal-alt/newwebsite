About Us

About CSC E-SEWA SOCIETY

CSC E-SEWA SOCIETY, founded in 2015 and headquartered in Punjab, is a mission-driven organization dedicated to skill development, education, employment support, rural empowerment, consultancy, and capacity building. The organization works to empower individuals with job-ready skills and sustainable livelihood opportunities.
